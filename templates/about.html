About AgriSens

AgriSens is a modern, AI-powered platform designed to empower farmers and agronomists with actionable insights for crop management and plant health. Leveraging advanced machine learning and deep learning, AgriSens provides real-time weather forecasting, plant disease detection from images, and intelligent crop recommendations tailored to your local conditions.

How It Works

  • Plant Disease Detection: Upload a photo of a plant leaf. Our deep learning model (TensorFlow/Keras) analyzes the image to identify diseases and provides a diagnosis, along with AI-generated recommendations and next steps using the latest LLMs.
  • Crop Recommendation: Enter your soil and environmental data. AgriSens uses a machine learning model trained on agricultural datasets to suggest the most suitable crops for your land, maximizing yield and sustainability.
  • Weather Forecast: Access real-time, location-based weather forecasts to plan your farming activities and reduce risk.
  • AI Chatbot: Get instant answers to your agricultural queries, from best practices to troubleshooting, powered by advanced language models.

Why AgriSens?

  • Modern, mobile-friendly UI with a consistent theme
  • AI-driven insights for smarter, faster decisions
